ELGE

Emergence lane generator

  • Dynamically generates a lane for the emergency vehicles such as Ambulance and Fire Brigades.
  • Uses Clustering algorithms to tag the vehicles on road.
  • It enables the care-taker to effectively manage the patient.
  • Based on client-server model.
  • The vehicles get a message on their dash board to move in the specified direction and generate the lane at shortest and minimum number of vehicle movements.

Publications

Block graph of a Graph

  • Published in Vladikavkaz Mathematical Journal (Russian Journal).
  • A block graph B(G) of a graph G, is the graph whose vertices are the blocks of G and in which two vertices are adjacent whenever the corresponding blocks have a cut-vertex in common.
  • This Paper aims to study the properties of Block graphs and present the characterization of the graphs whose corresponding graphs are planar, maximally outer-planar, minimally non-outerplanar, Eulerian and Hamiltonian.
  • Paper aims to study the properties of Block Graphs and its Characteristics.
